## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Day 1 Success

1. **Practice out loud, not in your head**: Writing is different from speaking. Muscle memory for spoken words is crucial.

2. **Record yourself**: Listen back. You'll hear filler words, pacing issues, and confidence gaps that you can't see when speaking.

3. **Practice with someone**: A friend or colleague can give real feedback. Ask them: "Did you understand my story? Was it compelling?"

4. **Explain to non-QA people**: If you can explain STLC to a non-technical person, you can explain it to an interviewer.

5. **Use the mirror**: Practice eye contact, hand gestures, facial expressions. Confidence shows physically.

6. **Time yourself obsessively**: Get comfortable with your timing. Too fast = nervousness. Too slow = rambling.

7. **Sleep well**: The night before your interview, get 8 hours. Cognitive performance and confidence depend on sleep.

8. **Eat well**: Don't interview hungry or over-caffeinated. Stable energy = stable performance.

9. **Dress to impress**: Physical confidence affects mental confidence. Wear something that makes you feel professional.

10. **Breathe**: Before answering, take one deep breath. Calms nerves, oxygenates brain, improves articulation.
